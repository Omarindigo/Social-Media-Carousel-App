Keyboard Controls and Gestures

  • Left Arrow (←): Rewind 10 seconds
  • Right Arrow (→): Forward 10 seconds
  • Up Arrow (↑): Increase volume
  • Down Arrow (↓): Decrease volume
  • Spacebar: Play/Pause video
  • < or ,: Previous video
  • > or .: Next video
  • Ctrl: Show native video controls
  • Swipe Left/Right: Change videos
  • Swipe Up/Down: Adjust volume
  • Drag Up: Show native video controls
  • Double-tap: Forward/Rewind 10 seconds
  • / or ?: Show this help modal
